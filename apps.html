My Final Project: Blackjack

Here is the Blackjack card game that I coded in the Khan Adademy ProcessingJS library. To play the game you first need to click the start button. From there you will see the dealers cards and your own cards. You can decide whether or not you would like to hit (take another card) or stand (compare cards with the dealer) using the buttons at the bottom of the screen. The dealer is programmed only to hit if his total is under 17. To win make sure your total is greater than the dealers, but not over 21. Any total over 21 results in a loss. Enjoy!
